Community Standards

By participating in ACRP, you agree to follow:

  • Our Terms of Service
  • Our Privacy Policy
  • Discord Terms of Service
  • Roblox Terms of Use

Failure to comply with these may result in removal from the community.

Discord Rules

By joining ACRP, you agree to Discord's Terms of Service & Community Guidelines and our server rules. Non-compliance may result in warnings, mutes, or bans depending on severity.

  • Spam and Trolling Prevention: No spamming or trolling.
  • Universal Respect: No harassment, hate speech, or derogatory remarks.
  • ACRP-Focused Content: Keep discussions related to ACRP.
  • No Unauthorized Promotions: No self-promotion or advertising without staff permission.
  • Channel Etiquette: Use channels for their intended purposes.
  • Drama-Free Environment: Avoid drama; report issues privately.
  • Safe Content Only: No NSFW content.
  • Following Staff Instructions: Follow staff guidance at all times.
  • Considerate Use of Mentions: Use mentions thoughtfully and sparingly.
  • We Moderate DMs: DMs are moderated for advertising, NSFW, and TOS violations.

Enjoy your time and help us keep ACRP a welcoming, creative, and respectful community!

Roblox / In-Game Rules

Banned Vehicles

  • Banned Civilian Vehicles: Strugatti Ettore, Falcon Heritage, Surrey 650S, Takeo Experience, Navara Horizon, Averon R8, Leland LTS5-V Blackwing, Bullhorn Determinator SFP Fury, Bullhorn Prancer Widebody
  • Banned Police Vehicles: 2022 Bullhorn Determinator SFP Fury (LE), Bullhorn Prancer Pursuit Widebody, Chevlon Corbeta TZ, 2011 Chevlon Amigo ZL1, Falcon Stallion 350, SWAT Truck
  • Banned Fire and EMS Vehicles: Tanker, Heavy Rescue, Special Operations Unit, Medical Bus, Heavy Tanker, FD Mobile Command Center
  • Banned DOT Vehicles: Cone Truck, Street Sweeper, Salt Truck

In-Game Rules

  • Management may take action without due process. Appeals are allowed.
  • TOS & Banned RPs: Suicide and Bomb RP are strictly prohibited. Kidnap/Hostage RP is allowed (no torture). No inappropriate minor/underage RP. Roblox TOS violations = ban.
  • Staff: Follow all staff orders. No running from, impersonating, or killing staff.
  • Alt Accounts: Not allowed unless given permission.
  • No Intention to Roleplay (NITRP): Messing around = kick/ban.
  • Full Killing: Wait for EMS or suspects to leave before respawning.
  • RDM/VDM: No random killing or ramming.
  • Guns: All banned guns are disabled.
  • Driving: Drive realistically; no erratic driving.
  • Accidents: Crashes above 30 mph must be roleplayed.
  • Cop Baiting & Priority Calls: No cop baiting. Panic button/radio spam is not allowed.
  • Cuff & Jail Rush: Roleplay cuffing and transporting suspects.
  • Avatars/Uniforms/Liveries: Use only realistic avatars, server uniforms, and custom liveries.
  • Protesting: Must have permission, be non-violent, and not about race/politics.
  • Groups: 3+ people required for major robberies.
  • Permission: Major calls require staff permission.
  • Interferences: Only engage in RPs if involved from the start.
  • Safe Zones: No major calls at spawn, police/fire/DOT stations, or hospital.
  • NLR: New-Life Rule applies after death, hospital, or jail.
  • Fear RP: Act as if your life is in danger during Fear RP.
  • Animal RP: Prohibited, including K9 RP.
  • Priorities: Approval required for major priorities (bank/jewelry robbery, escorts, hostage, high-speed pursuits, protests). See #üî´‚îÉPriority Request and fill out the form.

By following these rules, you help keep ACRP safe, fun, and fair for everyone. Thank you for being part of our community!
