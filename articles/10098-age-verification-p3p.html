Introducing Age Checks (Phase 3 Plans)

Updated: 2/10/26

Updates on ACRP's rollout of AI-Powered age verification and their plans for the future.

Hey Everyone,

We wanted to share some information to provide some transparency regarding the rollout of AI-powered age verification.

What is in the works?

  • Age Assurance
    We are developing additional technologies to help ensure that a user is at least the age of 13. It will work by analyzing the messages a user sends and putting them against previous messages that they have sent (specifically within the past month of the message we are looking at), and training data, and we will use this to generate a confidence score on whether we think you are 13 or older. If we find that messages within a certain time span have a low confidence score, then we will prompt an age verification via Didit ®. If you pass the checks, then your messages (specifically the ones that triggered the check) will be analyzed, and we will remember that this is how you talk.
  • A Safety Assurance Council
    We are working on a Safety Assurance Council to help us respect users' privacy while helping maintain a safer community for all. Members of this council will give us ideas and feedback on ideas for AI safety systems. Are you interested in being a member? Stay tuned for the application.
  • An Appeal System For Those Who Were Flagged For Being Under 13 Falsely
    Currently, when users are flagged as under 13, they will be automatically banned. We are developing an appeal system where users who were falsely banned can provide their ID, and they can challenge the determination.
  • A way for users to verify their age without having to provide a facial scan (Via Didit ®)
    So… Actually.. Thats it. Its self explanatory… Anyway.

What's the plan now?

With the current age checks, we will mandate that all users complete a successful check by 3/10/2026. If a user does not complete these checks by that date, then they will not be able to interact with the community in any way until verification is completed. This includes but is not limited to:

  • You will not be able to see ANY channels except Announcements
  • You will not be able to read, send, or react to any messages

These restrictions will be in place until you complete the verification process.

What about my data?

We are using https://didit.me for Age Assurance and facial scans. We understand that some users may have concerns about this provider. At this time, Didit reports no known history of data breaches. You may review their privacy practices here: https://didit.me/terms/privacy-policy/

You may view our privacy practices at https://alleghenycountyroleplay.com/privacy and our terms and conditions at https://alleghenycountyroleplay.com/terms

We appreciate all users' feedback, which is why you can email us at ownership@acrp.work with any questions, comments, or concerns about the new policies.

FAQ For Age Verification.

  • How can I ensure an accurate reading of my face?
    Please ensure that the lighting around you is bright and shines directly on your face. Avoid having bright lights behind or below you, as they can create glare or shadows. Position the camera at eye level, point it directly at your face, and look into the lens. Make sure all filters and effects are disabled so your image looks natural and avoids looking like a deepfake. Make sure that you're the only person in frame. Keep your movements slow and steady while taking selfies. Remove any masks, sunglasses, or any other face coverings or accessories.
  • How can I ensure an accurate reading of my ID?
    Take a clear, glare-free photo of your ID that contains a solid background. Ensure that your ID is positioned straight and fully within frame.
