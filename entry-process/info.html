Welcome to Allegheny County Roleplay

We are a whitelisted community, originally semi-whitelisted and founded in 2024. We have much to offer like the Pittsburgh Police Department, Allegheny County Sheriff's Office, Pennsylvania State Police, PennDOT, and much more.

Requirements to Become Whitelisted

•
Age Requirement: You MUST be 14 years or older (exceptions can be made on a case-by-case basis)
•
Technical Requirements: You MUST play ERLC on a computer or laptop and be able to run ER:LC smoothly with a minimum of 15 FPS
•
Language Proficiency: You MUST speak fluent English to ensure clear communication during roleplay scenarios
•
Communication Equipment: You must have a working microphone and be willing to actively communicate during roleplay sessions

Ready to Join Our Community?

Take the first step towards becoming part of our professional roleplay community. Our application process is designed to ensure the best experience for all members.

You may apply ahead of time online, but you MUST join our Discord server to complete the process. The Discord server is essential for community communication, updates, and coordination of roleplay sessions.
